Red-black (balanced) trees

Widely used in the wild: java.util.TreeMap, java.util.TreeSet

Used for file systems and databases: HFS, HFS+, PostgreSQL, EXT3FS, JFS, NTFS, Oracle, DB2

B-Trees

Similar to 2-3 Trees except there's up to M - 1 key-link pairs per node.

Can get to any piece of data (dynamic search symbol table), even if trillions of elements, with only 5 or 6 probes.
